Learn how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 kilos hominy corn
  • 100 grams cocoa
  • Granulated piloncillo
  • Water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. On a griddle, toast cocoa until charred and until peel comes off easily. Peel cocoa with your hands and set aside.
  2. Transfer hominy corn and cocoa to a metate (mealing stone) or a food processor and grind to a paste.
  3. Place paste in a bowl, add water, and dissolve with your hands.
  4. Once dissolved, add ice and sweeten homemade pozol.
